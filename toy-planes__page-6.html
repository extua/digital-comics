Toy Planes, page 6

  • Previous
  • Next
Panel 1: EXT. Beach dominates. In the distance, gantry, palm trees, balloon platform that cradles the rocketplane.
ANNOUNCER Launch in 10... 9... 8...
Panel 2: CLOSEUP Balloons.
ANNOUNCER 7... 6...
NARRATOR It was the cheapest way to orbit. Balloon up as high as you could on a triangular platform, the light the rocketplane and head for orbit.
Panel 3: EXT. Rocketplane.
ANNOUNCER 5... 4...
NARRATOR We scavenged everything from companies about to go out of business. Plane was from a Chinese corporation. Guidance systems are all open source.
Panel 4: INT. Closeup of NARRATOR's helmet
ANNOUNCER 3... 2... 1...
NARRATOR Online betting parlors have our odds at fifty percent. We're not the first to try this, but we're the first island nation.
Panel 5: EXT. Rocketplane (can reuse from other panels if needed)
ANNOUNCER Launch!
Panel 6: EXT. Balloon platform rises from vantage point of beach. EXT. Above clouds, rocket fires and leaps out of platform.
Panel 7:INT. Blue band of earth, curve of space can be seen through porthole. NARRATOR is holding spaceplane toy to his chest, out of package.
NARRATOR Hello out there, all of you. We're coming up too.
Optional final EXT: toy plane floating in darkness of space by itself.
