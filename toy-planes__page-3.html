I want ask you something. All that money you spending for that rocket by the beach. You don't think it better spent on new roads. Or more schools?
Only a piece of it's funded by our government.
For the rest we got private investors and corporate sponsors.
Panel 2: EXT. Satellite shot of island from space, with tech numbers and false colors overlaying. Dialogue laid over.
"How many people live on our island? Tens of thousands?"
"Our food is imported from countries that use satellites to help them farm better..."
"...that use spinoff technologies developed from studying recycling that you need in space."
Panel 3: INT. Driver looks dubious.
Why wait for other nations to patent technology first.
Good things come to those who research and invent things.
Maybe.
Panel 4: EXT. Mini-bus busting through the roads in Caribbean traffic. (To save time you could delete this EXT and just add a third word bubble to previous INT with 'yes')
You still want dropped off by the market?
Yes.
