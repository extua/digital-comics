Toy Planes

Page 1

Panel 1: EXT. Beach scene, with private space rocket near the beach. There is a space-plane at the top of rocket. Palm tree off to the side. Stained with use, not new looking. Repurposed.
Panel 2: INT. Closeup of hair clippers, technology contrasts with rocket details. Dreadlocked hair.
Panel 3: INT. JOANIE, a dreadlocked brown woman with a bemused expression is about to dip the clippers into the hair. NARRATOR is a middle aged, mixed race Caribbean man with dreadlocks. JOANIE You grew these at college when you went to the states. You sure you want me doing this? NARRATOR I was starting to lose my accent. I didn't want to forget where I came from. JOANIE Then you really sure about this? NARRATOR The companies sponsoring the rocket-plane want them cut.
Panel 4: EXT. Close up of the private space rocket and plane, or possibly of the interior (if interior, show a generic can of soda on a console of advanced looking switches). NARRATOR's words continue over the top. NARRATOR They say it would be a disaster for a helmet to not seal properly. Explosive decompression is not something a soda company wants their customers associating with their brand.
Panel 5: INT. JOANIE smirks. JOANIE Ras. How come you ain't insulted they don't think you smart enough to make sure you know what you doing.
Panel 6: INT. He leans forward, his head in his hands, as JOANIE shaves head. (Alternate, a close up of locks hitting ground) NARRATOR But we need their money, sis. For now. So let's just do it.
